The Reflector

Stay up to date with my newsletter here. My publishing schedule isn’t as consistent as I’d like. In a perfect world I would publish a newsletter weekly through this link: https://substack.com/@patricknafziger Subscribe or follow along as you’d like. My reflections tend to be based on scripture but just as often I reflect on some minor absurdity and try to find a hidden gem that I believe is worth carrying through the week. I find it helpful to try to find or create some beauty regularly in this dark world. For what it’s worth, my paid subscribers on Substack do receive little extras every now and then-I try to make it worth your while.
